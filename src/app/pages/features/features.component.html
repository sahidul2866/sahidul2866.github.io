Capabilities

Features & dashboards

Core features, advanced analytics, and ML-enhanced options from the proposal.

Core features

  • Duplicate detection by tax ID, name, address, email.
  • Vendor & AP history (invoices, payments, reviews) across fiscal years.
  • KPIs: total spend, vendors with no spend, excluded vendors.
  • Advanced filtering/sorting by ERP, FY, vendor group, spend.
  • Server-side pagination for large datasets.
  • Export to Excel/CSV; import reviewer notes.
  • Security & roles with SSO and row-level access.
  • Audit trail for reviews/changes; scalable to new ERPs.

Advanced analytics & stats

  • Statistical spend profiling: mean/median/variance/std across vendors/categories/ERPs/FYs.
  • Anomaly & outlier detection (IQR, Z-score + ML) for invoice amounts, payment frequency, duplicate payments.
  • Vendor risk scoring: missing tax ID, inconsistent names, bank mismatches, reversals, duplicate likelihood.
  • Payment pattern analysis: unusual terms, same-day/recurring/split payments.
  • GL-level trends & variance; aging buckets 0–30/31–60/61–90/90+.
  • Spend seasonality and invoice–payment lifecycle stats (avg days to pay, late/early, credit memos).
  • User activity analytics for reviewer productivity and audit traceability.

ML-enhanced options

  • ML duplicate vendor detection with embeddings/similarity (≈98% accuracy).
  • AI vendor classification (Gov/Bank/University/Individual/Non-profit/Healthcare, etc.).
  • AI-assisted reason suggestions for exclude/review decisions.
  • Predictive spend forecasting from historical AP.
  • Fraud probability model (addresses, reversals, split invoices).
  • Smart semantic search (“vendors with repeated small invoices”).

Executive dashboards

  • Spend overview: total spend, top 10 vendors, variance by FY, GL breakdown, ERP-wise spend.
  • Vendor health: risk score, completeness, diversity, duplicate probability, missing fields.
  • AP process: days-to-pay, late %, reversals, credit memos, invoice lifecycle.
  • Audit activity: reviewed/pending rows, comments, reviewer performance.
  • Duplicate analysis: grouped vendors, top risky groups, false positives, exclusions.
