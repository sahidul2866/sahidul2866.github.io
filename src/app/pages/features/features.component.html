Quick snapshots of feature inventory for stakeholders.

Duplicate detection

  • Identify potential duplicate vendors and invoices using configurable rules and fuzzy matching.
  • Group duplicates with clear reasons (same TIN, name + address, email match).
  • Allow audit/data teams to review, flag, or export duplicate groups and invoices.

Dashboards and metrics

  • Visualize vendor and AP statistics: total spend, number of vendors, top vendors by spend.
  • Trend and fiscal year comparisons with breakdowns by company, region, or ERP.
  • Data quality metrics for missing TINs, invalid addresses, and incomplete contact info.

Audit-ready exports

  • Standardized export formats for duplicate vendor/invoice packs and vendor profiles with enriched attributes.
  • Transaction listings filtered by company, date range, or risk flag.
  • Designed to be audit-ready directly from live Snowflake data.

Data quality tools

  • Standardize and clean vendor names, addresses, and TIN formats.
  • Validation checks for phone numbers, postal codes, and email formats.
  • Flags for incomplete or inconsistent vendor records.

Central Vendor Enrichment System

  • Cross-client vendor layer with signals from multiple Bedrock clients plus selected public/third-party sources.
  • Produces a 360Â° view of each vendor to boost duplicate detection and risk/compliance checks.
  • Keeps client transaction data siloed while sharing vendor identity metadata.

Role-based access & security

  • Access profiles for data, audit, and leadership teams; row-level security where required.
  • SSO-based authentication and authorization with full logging of user actions.
  • Supports governance, traceability, and defensibility for audits.

Future roadmap (Phase 2+)

  • Predictive analytics: forecast vendor spend, cash flow/exposure, and predict data quality risks.
  • Anomaly detection: flag unusual payment patterns, vendor behavior shifts, and enable anomaly review workflows.
  • Supplier risk scoring: composite vendor risk score with prioritization and risk dashboards.
  • Self-service rules & automation: configurable rules engine plus scheduled checks and alerts.
  • Integration & productization: deeper ERP integrations, client feedback loops, and tiered product packages.
