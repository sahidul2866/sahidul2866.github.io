Roles, risks, and recommendations

Ownership, mitigations, and team model from the proposal.

Governance & security guardrails

  • Client financial data remains siloed; only vendor identity metadata is shared across clients.
  • Vendors are tagged as Validated, Pending Validation, or Client-Specific.
  • Row-level access, PII protection, and client opt-in policies enforced in Snowflake.
  • Full logging of user actions for governance and traceability.

Risks & mitigations

  • Complex ERP variations: Implement standardized staging templates.
  • Performance challenges: Use Snowflake clustering and caching.
  • Security compliance/PII exposure: Apply data masking and role-based policies.

Team recommendation

Engage a contractual development team while core audit teams stay focused on governance and review.

  • Snowflake Data Engineer (1) – 4–6 months; build CUR_VM/CUR_AP schemas and pipelines.
  • Backend API Developer (1) – 4 months; create REST APIs and integrations.
  • Frontend Developer (1) – 4 months; develop the portal interface.

Plan for knowledge handover to the Bedrock data team post-launch to sustain improvements.
