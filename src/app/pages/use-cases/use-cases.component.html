Scenario 1

Duplicate vendor review for audit

Audit-ready
  • Before: Manual ERP extracts, ad-hoc SQL and Excel deduping; every scope change means rework.
  • After: Portal filters (client/date/rule set) run standardized duplicate logic on CUR_VM/CUR_AP; duplicate groups and audit-ready exports in hours.

Scenario 2

Missing or invalid TIN clean-up

Data quality
  • Before: Excel filters and ad-hoc formulas to fix formats; little reuse of prior work.
  • After: Data Quality dashboard shows TIN completeness/format KPIs; CUR_VM standardizes formats; Central Vendor System reuses validated TINs and outputs a remediation list.

Scenario 3

Cross-client vendor visibility

Master Vendor Repository
  • Before: Separate Excel/SQL per engagement; manual searches and inconsistent matching rules.
  • After: Central Vendor System maintains global identities; Vendor Overview shows where a vendor appears, high-level spend, and enriched sanctions/watchlist flags.

Scenario 4

Onboarding a new client engagement

Onboarding
  • Before: Custom mappings and SQL per engagement; weeks before reliable insight.
  • After: Standard RAW→SILVER→CUR_VM/CUR_AP pipeline with reusable transforms; Central Vendor System auto-matches vendors; portal quickly surfaces duplicates, spend metrics, and data quality issues.

Scenario 5

Management question: top vendors and spend trend

Spend overview
  • Before: Custom queries or client reports exported to Excel; pivot tables rebuilt for every follow-up.
  • After: Spend Overview dashboard with filters by date/company/region, instant top vendors and trends, and one-click export of underlying or summarized data.
