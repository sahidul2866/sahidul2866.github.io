Project Overview

Bedrock VM/AP Analytics Portal

Purpose, background, and why the portal matters.

Purpose

Replace slow, manual, Excel-based VM/AP analysis with a centralized, secure, and scalable Snowflake-powered portal. Deliver instant duplicate detection, historical spend visibility, and audit-ready reporting with zero manual file transfers.

Background & Current Challenges

  • Analysts run SQL manually across ERPs; results exported to Excel and emailed.
  • No version control or data lineage; audit reviews tracked separately.
  • ERP extracts too large for Excel; incomplete datasets and slow analysis.
  • Security risk from emailed vendor/payment data; PII exposure potential.
  • Every ERP needs different logic; hard to standardize or automate.
  • No permanent audit trail; weak defensibility during audits.

Why build the portal

  • Centralize data in Snowflake as a single source of truth.
  • Real-time duplicate detection, spend metrics, and audit dashboards.
  • Self-service analytics for auditors/managers without SQL.
  • Reduce processing time and manual errors by 60%+.
  • Establish traceability, governance, and security compliance.

Non-technical summary

Secure login, live Snowflake data, interactive dashboards, browser-based filtering/commenting/export, full audit logging.

“No more Excel chaos, no more SQL re-runs — just clean, auditable data available 24/7.”
