Shareable timeline + deliverables for steering meetings.

Proposed timeline

  • Phase 1 – Discovery & Design 3 weeks

    Stakeholder workshops, requirements, data contracts, and UI mockups.

  • Phase 2 – Data Foundation 4 weeks

    Snowflake RAW→SILVER→CURATED schemas, multi-ERP ingestion, staging templates.

  • Phase 3 – API & Duplicate Engine 5 weeks

    Core logic and endpoints: REST APIs, duplicate detection rules, and audit logging.

  • Phase 4 – Frontend MVP 4 weeks

    Portal shell, dashboards, filters, and export/import flows.

  • Phase 5 – Security Setup 2 weeks

    SSO, masking, and role-based access.

  • Phase 6 – Master Vendor Repository Development 4 weeks

    Schema design, validation rules, and integration into portal views.

  • Phase 7 – Pilot & QA 3 weeks

    Testing, feedback, refinements.

  • Phase 8 – Launch & Training 2 weeks

    Rollout, documentation, training.

  • Phase 9 – Continuous Improvement Ongoing

    Enhancements and predictive features.

Expected deliverables

  • Unified VM/AP schema in Snowflake (CUR_VM, CUR_AP, DUP tables).
  • Master Vendor Repository with validation logic.
  • Web portal with dashboards, filters, and export/import options.
  • Integrated duplicate detection and history lookup.
  • Role-based security with full audit logs.
  • Documentation and user training materials.
